# EmberSoul Labs Product Roadmap

This roadmap records all planned products while keeping the current development focus on EmberOS V1.

## Priority Table

| Priority | Product | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| P1 | EmberOS | V1 in development | Current primary focus. Ship V1 first. |
| P2 | EmberCore ERP | Approximately 50% completed | Existing product asset. Continue after EmberOS V1 launch priority is stable. |
| P3 | EmberOS V2-V4 | Planned | Future enhancements only. Must not affect V1 launch. |
| P4 | AI Job Agent | Product concept | Main focus is helping job seekers save time through resume optimization, matching, automatic applications and tracking. |
| P5 | Ember Mind | Product concept | Lowest priority. Emotional companion, journaling and wellbeing. |

## Development Rule

Only EmberOS V1 is allowed to affect current development decisions.

All other products and future versions are recorded here for long-term planning and must not delay V1 launch.
