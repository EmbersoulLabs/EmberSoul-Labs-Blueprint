# FOUNDATION-001 Brand System

Status: Locked

## Purpose

Records the accepted Brand System Product Decisions for EmberSoul Labs and EmberOS.

Only accepted Product Decisions are listed as Product Decisions.

## Product Decisions

### FOUNDATION-001-001 Company

Company:

- EmberSoul Labs

### FOUNDATION-001-002 Tagline

Tagline:

- Ignite the Soul of Every Small Business

### FOUNDATION-001-003 Brand Positioning

Brand positioning:

- Building practical AI that helps people live and work more easily.

### FOUNDATION-001-004 Brand Belief

Brand belief:

- AI should adapt to people, not people to AI.

### FOUNDATION-001-005 Product Philosophy

Product philosophy:

- Make complex business workflows simple through thoughtful software and AI.

### FOUNDATION-001-006 Product Naming

Independent products follow:

- Ember + One English Word

Examples:

- EmberOS
- EmberCore
- EmberMind

### FOUNDATION-001-007 Module Naming

Modules are not independent products.

Examples inside EmberOS:

- Business Profile
- Campaign
- Video Studio
- CEO Agent
- Marketing Package

### FOUNDATION-001-008 Target Users

Primary target users:

- Small Business
- Retail
- Florist
- Cafe
- Restaurant
- Agency
- Service Business
- Freelancer
- Creator

### FOUNDATION-001-009 AI Visibility

Users know AI is working.

Users should not need to know:

- Provider
- Model
- Temperature
- Tokens

### FOUNDATION-001-010 Human Control

AI never makes final business decisions.

Rules:

- Users approve.
- AI executes.

### FOUNDATION-001-011 Automation Principle

Users make the decision.

AI executes the workflow.

Rules:

- AI follows the latest user-approved instruction until changed.
- AI never assumes changes to user intent.

## Architect Recommendations

No additional architect recommendations are recorded as accepted Product Decisions in this foundation record.

FOUNDATION-001 Brand System is Locked.
