# Marketing Score Prompt

## Purpose

Evaluate marketing effectiveness of generated campaign assets.

## Score Dimensions

- Video Quality
- Editing Quality
- Marketing Quality
- Brand Match
- Viewer Perspective

## Output

- Marketing Score
- Success Prediction
- AI Confidence
- Top Improvement
- Strengths
- Risks
- Recommended Actions

## Rules

- Score marketing effectiveness.
- Success Prediction measures marketing potential, not guaranteed views, likes, sales, or revenue.
