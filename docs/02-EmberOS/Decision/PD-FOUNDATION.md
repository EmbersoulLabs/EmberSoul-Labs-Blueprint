## User Decisions

### PD-FOUNDATION-001 Brand System

FOUNDATION-001 Brand System is Locked.

Accepted Product Decisions:

- Company is EmberSoul Labs.
- Tagline is Ignite the Soul of Every Small Business.
- Brand positioning is: Building practical AI that helps people live and work more easily.
- Brand belief is: AI should adapt to people, not people to AI.
- Product philosophy is: Make complex business workflows simple through thoughtful software and AI.
- Independent products follow Ember + One English Word.
- Modules are not independent products.
- Primary target users are Small Business, Retail, Florist, Cafe, Restaurant, Agency, Service Business, Freelancer, and Creator.
- Users know AI is working, but should not need to know provider, model, temperature, or tokens.
- AI never makes final business decisions. Users approve. AI executes.
- Users make the decision. AI executes the workflow.
- AI follows the latest user-approved instruction until changed.
- AI never assumes changes to user intent.

### PD-FOUNDATION-002 Design System

FOUNDATION-002 Design System is Locked.

Accepted Product Decisions:

- Core Design Principles are added.
- Brand Canvas is Dark Professional Canvas.
- Brand Gradient is Blue -> Cyan -> Gold.
- Primary Brand Symbol is Ember Flame.
- Colors communicate meaning and must not be used only for decoration.
- Functional colors are Primary, Success, Warning, Error, and Neutral.
- Motion is minimal and only supports communication.
- Icons support text.
- Primary navigation must include text labels.
- Each page should have only one Primary Action.
- Business modules should be separated into reusable Cards.
- Every Empty State should explain why there is no data, what the next step is, and provide a CTA.
- Every screen should answer: Where am I? What's happening? What's next?
- Good design makes the next step obvious.
- One Workspace. Any Device.
- Desktop, Tablet and Mobile provide the same product. Only presentation adapts.
- People change devices. Their work should not have to.
- Prefer interactions users already understand.
- AI components use one consistent AI visual language.
- Ember Flame is the AI identity.
- AI evaluates quality and does not guarantee business outcomes.
- AI assessment and scores must show the required prediction disclaimer.
- AI must explain recommendations before asking users to accept changes.
- Success feedback explains what completed and the next step.
- Error feedback explains what happened and what users can do.
- Background AI jobs must display progress, estimated time, and notifications.
- Users should always know whether a request was received, what the system is doing, whether they need to wait, and what happens next.
- Once users submit a task, the system should protect it.
- If AI services are temporarily unavailable, queue the request and resume automatically once services recover.
- The Never Lose User Work UX copy is accepted as a Product Decision.
