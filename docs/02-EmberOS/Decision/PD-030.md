# PD-030 - Business Social Links

Status:
Locked

Decision:
Social Links are optional in Business Profile.

Rules:
- Store Website, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube, Xiaohongshu, and WhatsApp Business when provided.
- Social Links may support future Auto Publish, CTA, and Analytics features.
- Missing Social Links must not block Campaign generation.

Reason:
Social channels are useful business context and future publishing targets, but not every business uses every platform.
