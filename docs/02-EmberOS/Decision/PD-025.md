# PD-025 - Company Name Modification Rules

Status:
Locked

Decision:
Company Name is editable in Business Profile.

Rules:
- User may edit Company Name.
- EmberOS must warn user that changes may affect future AI outputs.
- Existing Campaigns are not regenerated automatically.

Reason:
Company Name may change as small businesses refine their identity, but historical campaign outputs should remain stable unless explicitly regenerated.
