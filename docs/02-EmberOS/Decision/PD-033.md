# PD-033 - Business Hours Optional

Status:
Locked

Decision:
Business Hours are optional in Business Profile.

Rules:
- Business Hours are not required for V1.
- If provided, they may support Schedule Posting, Auto Reply, Marketing Timing, and profile completeness.
- Missing Business Hours must not block Campaign generation.

Reason:
Many freelancers and small business owners do not operate with fixed business hours.
