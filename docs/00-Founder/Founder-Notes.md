# Founder Notes

## FN-001: Sustainable Company Vision

The founder does not aim to build a company with thousands of employees.

The goal is to build a practical AI software company that can create real value, generate stable recurring income, and operate with low burden and zero debt.

A practical success target is at least SGD 10,000 monthly income with zero debt.

## FN-002: Why EmberOS Exists

SME owners are often responsible for sales, customer service, purchasing, operations, delivery, admin work and staff management.

Marketing is important, but it is usually pushed to the end because owners have no time to plan content, edit videos, write captions or review performance.

EmberOS exists to become the AI Marketing Team for small businesses.

## FN-003: Real User Pain

The user has already done the hardest part when they record a video.

EmberOS should take over the remaining marketing work: planning, editing, captions, hashtags and reporting.

## FN-004: Product Philosophy Across EmberSoul Labs

EmberSoul Labs products should help people and small businesses gain access to capabilities that were previously available only to larger organizations or professional teams.
