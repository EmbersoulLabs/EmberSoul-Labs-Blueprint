# Marketing Angle Prompt Specification

## Purpose

Determine what the campaign should communicate based on the business, campaign and content analysis.

Marketing Angle is the bridge between analysis and generation.

## Input

- Business Profile
- Campaign Objective
- Content Analysis
- Output Language

## Output

Structured JSON including:

- Marketing Objective
- Primary Marketing Angle
- Reasoning
- Audience interpretation
- Expected business impact
- Suggested hook direction
- Suggested subtitle direction
- Suggested caption direction

## Rules

- Do not copy Business Strategy directly into subtitles or captions.
- Do not use generic business terms as viewer-facing text.
- Viewer-facing content must be based on the Marketing Angle.
- The report can explain the business reasoning, but video content must be audience-facing.

## V1 Scope

Marketing Angle decides what the campaign should say. It does not render video or create the final report directly.
