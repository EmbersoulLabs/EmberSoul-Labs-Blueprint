# Marketing Report Prompt Specification

## Purpose

Generate a clear marketing report for SME owners who do not have a dedicated marketing team.

## Input

- Business Profile
- Campaign Objective
- Content Analysis
- Marketing Angle
- Generated assets
- Marketing Score

## Output

The report must answer:

1. Who is the target audience?
2. Why is this plan recommended?
3. What business impact is expected?

Additional sections:

- Marketing Score
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Suggested improvements
- Platform caption summary
- Hashtag summary

## Rules

- Marketing Report is for the business owner.
- It can explain strategy and business reasoning.
- It must not be directly reused as subtitles or viewer-facing copy.
- Keep language practical and easy to understand for SME owners.

## V1 Scope

The report should help the owner understand what to post, why to post it and how to improve.
