## PD-010 — Business Profile Required Fields

Decision:

Business Profile requires:

- Company Name
- Industry
- Services
- Region

Reason:

These fields give EmberOS the minimum stable business context required for AI marketing analysis.

Impact:

Marketing outputs should become less generic and more aligned with the actual business.

---

## PD-011 — Brand Voice

Decision:

Brand Voice belongs to Campaign configuration and supports multi-select.

Reason:

A business may use different voices for different campaigns.

Impact:

Campaign setup becomes more flexible without forcing one fixed brand tone across all content.

---

## PD-012 — Low Confidence Confirmation Flow

Decision:

AI may infer missing Business Profile information, but low-confidence inference must require user confirmation.

Reason:

AI guesses can be useful, but unconfirmed guesses should not become final product truth.

Impact:

The product reduces hallucination risk and improves user trust.

---

## PD-013 — AI Brain Thinking Order

Decision:

AI reasoning order is:

1. Video
2. User Description
3. Campaign
4. Business Profile
5. AI Reasoning

Reason:

Video is direct evidence. User description clarifies meaning. Campaign defines objective. Business Profile provides background. AI reasoning should happen after those inputs are evaluated.

Impact:

The system becomes more evidence-driven and less dependent on static profile assumptions.

---

## PD-014 — Marketing Score Framework

Decision:

Marketing Score includes:

- Video Quality
- Editing Quality
- Marketing Quality
- Brand Match
- Viewer Perspective

Reason:

Marketing performance depends on content quality, editing clarity, persuasion, brand alignment, and viewer experience.

Impact:

Scores become explainable and actionable.

---

## PD-015 — Critical Error Detection

Decision:

EmberOS should detect critical understanding errors and recommend regeneration when they occur.

Critical cases:

- Product identification is wrong
- Business Profile has serious conflict with content
- Audience is wrong
- Content analysis failed
- Critical Understanding Error is detected

Reason:

Some errors are too severe for minor editing. Regeneration is safer than patching a flawed analysis.

Impact:

The product protects output quality and prevents users from polishing fundamentally wrong content.

---

## PD-016 — Marketing Success Prediction V1

Decision:

Marketing Success Prediction V1 predicts Marketing Potential only.

It must not predict:

- Views
- Likes
- Revenue
- Orders

Reason:

External platform and market factors make exact performance prediction unreliable.

Impact:

EmberOS can guide marketing quality without overpromising measurable business outcomes.
